05 For Good

Technology should work for everyone.

Beyond the venture work, we build for people who can't pay. Developing nations, underserved communities, the ones that technology usually passes by. We believe the same tools we build at the frontier should be available to people on the ground.

Humanitarian projects don't follow an ROI model. They follow a different kind of math — reach, dignity, and access. We fund these with the commercial work, and we build them with the same intensity.
