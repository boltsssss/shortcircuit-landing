SHORT CIRCUIT Manifesto
ShortCircuit · Founding Document · 2026
The world runs
on systems.
Financial systems. Energy systems. Information systems.
Most systems evolve slowly.
But history does not.
Progress happens
through short circuits.
A short circuit bypasses the intended path.
Electricity finds a faster route. Technology does the same.
Every system, eventually, gets short-circuited.
Intelligence is becoming
infrastructure.
AI agents can now coordinate markets, execute financial strategies,
manage infrastructure, run businesses.
When intelligence becomes programmable, economic systems change.
New economic systems
will emerge.
Markets will run themselves.
Infrastructure will coordinate itself.
Capital will move programmatically.
The systems that enable this future are not companies. They are networks.
ShortCircuit exists
to build them.
Not to observe disruption. Not to invest in it.
To engineer it.
