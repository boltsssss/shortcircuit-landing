The World
Runs on
Systems.

Published 2026  ·  shortcircuit.ventures

The world runs
on systems.

  • Financial systems.
  • Energy systems.
  • Information systems.
  •  
  • Most systems evolve slowly.
  • But history does not.

Progress happens
through short circuits.

  • A short circuit bypasses the intended path.
  • Electricity finds a faster route.
  • Technology does the same.
  •  
  • The internet bypassed physical distribution.
  • Crypto bypassed institutional finance.
  • AI is bypassing human coordination.
  •  
  • Every system gets short-circuited.

Intelligence is becoming
infrastructure.

  • AI agents can now coordinate markets.
  • Execute financial strategies.
  • Manage infrastructure.
  • Run businesses.
  •  
  • When intelligence becomes programmable,
      economic systems change.

New economic systems
will emerge.

  • Markets will run themselves.
  • Infrastructure will coordinate itself.
  • Capital will move programmatically.
  •  
  • The systems that enable this future
  • are not companies.
  • They are networks.

ShortCircuit exists
to build them.

  • Not to observe disruption.
  • Not to invest in it.
  • To engineer it.
